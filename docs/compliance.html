Compliance Guide

Overview

This guide explains prohibited behaviour, examples of violations and the steps moderators take when a report is submitted.

Prohibited actions

  • Scamming / Fraudulent transactions
  • Distribution of malicious downloads or links
  • Unauthorized sale of accounts, cheats or stolen content
  • Harassment or doxxing other users

How to report

  1. Collect evidence (screenshots, URLs)
  2. Open a report via the Report an Issue page
  3. Moderators review and take action: warnings, removals or bans
